Going to a Declaration

See Also

To jump to the declaration for the class, function, or variable the cursor is on:

  • Right-click and choose Navigate > Go To Declaration.

If a C++ function is overridden by other methods, you can go to the declaration of the overriding methods. See Going to an Overridden or Overriding Method.
